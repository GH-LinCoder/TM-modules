How To Use

This is a mock help page. Click buttons to navigate.

  • The admin dashboard is always open on the left, next to the menu.
  • Click the admin/dash menu button to only see the dashboard (closes other pages)
  • To open any other page double click the menu button
  • Also double click its menu button to close the window
  • Single click the middle page button to swap the position of the 2nd & 3rd pages
  • The maximum number of open pages is the dashboard + 2 others
  • The How? button brings up these instructions
